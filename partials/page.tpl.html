Phase 1

Goal Setting

Develop level-appropriate goals for the year. Understand how sucess will be measured.

Roles and Responsibilities
Manager:
  • Share department goals with staff and discuss alignment with corporate goals
  • Reviews expectations with employees
  • Ensures employee goals are relivant and level-appropriate
  • Knows how employee sucess will be measured
  • Approve employee's goals
Employee:
  • Draft goals with department in mind
  • Refers to level charts to develop appropriate goals
  • Collaborates with teammates working on similar work
  • Shares goal with manager
